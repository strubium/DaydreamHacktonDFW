Welcome — Surface & Repair

You're in command of a stranded sub. The crew must gather and repair critical systems so you can surface.

How the game works:

  • Assign a crew member to a repair task (each task has a base time).
  • While repairing, crew take damage over time — damage is stronger now, so be careful.
  • Complete repairs to earn Supplies, which buy upgrades for crew.
  • Upgrades: Tool Kit (speed up repair), Reinforced Suit (reduce damage taken), and Med Training (improves Medic healing).
  • The Medic can be assigned to slowly heal other crew members. You can also buy an Auto-Heal Module so the Medic will automatically heal idle crew members.

Tip: Upgrading the Medic early helps keep your repairers alive while they work on the dangerous systems.
